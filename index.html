Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 24, 2026

1. Introduction

Abalone Marbles is a mobile board game developed by Abdelrahman Nabil Mohamed. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

By using Abalone Marbles, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

When you create an account or play, we may collect:

  • Email address (for registered accounts)
  • Display name / in-game name
  • Game statistics (wins, losses, streaks, marbles pushed)
  • In-app economy data (coins balance, daily spin history)
  • Device identifiers (for anonymous guest sessions)
  • Language preference and app settings

We do NOT collect: precise location, contacts, photos, or microphone data.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your game account
  • Save your progress, coins, and statistics
  • Enable online multiplayer matchmaking
  • Show your rank and achievements
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Send optional notifications (only if you enable them)

4. Third-Party Services

Abalone Marbles uses the following third-party services that may process your data according to their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore Database, Realtime Database) — stores your account data and game state. See: firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Google Analytics for Firebase — anonymous usage analytics to improve the app.
  • Google AdMob / Unity Ads — may display advertisements and collect advertising identifiers to show relevant ads. You can limit ad tracking in your device settings.

We do not sell your personal data to any third party.

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on Google Firebase servers with industry-standard encryption. We implement reasonable security measures to protect against unauthorized access.

Guest accounts store data locally on your device. Creating a registered account backs up your progress to the cloud.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. If you request account deletion, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at mr.3boody@gmail.com.

8. Children's Privacy

Abalone Marbles is rated 4+ and suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
